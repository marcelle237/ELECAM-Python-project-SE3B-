Voter Eligibility Information in Cameroon

Eligibility Criteria to Vote in Cameroon

To be eligible to vote in Cameroon, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a citizen of Cameroon.
  • Be at least 21 years old on or before election day.
  • Have a valid national identification card.
  • Not be under any legal voting disqualifications, such as a court ruling.

How to Obtain Your Voter’s Card

Follow these steps to obtain your voter’s card in Cameroon:

  1. Visit the nearest branch of Elections Cameroon (ELECAM) in your area.
  2. Present your valid national identification card.
  3. Provide personal details, including your full name, date of birth, and address.
  4. Have your photograph and fingerprints taken for registration.
  5. Receive a temporary voter registration receipt.
  6. Return to the ELECAM office after the processing period to collect your voter’s card.

Note: Registration is open during official voter registration periods, as announced by Elections Cameroon (ELECAM).
